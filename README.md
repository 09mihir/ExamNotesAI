<h1 align="center">ExamNotesAI</h1>

<p align="center">
  An AI-powered study assistant designed to help students generate smart notes, simplify complex topics, and prepare efficiently for exams.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>About The Project</h2>

<p>
ExamNotesAI is built to transform the traditional way of studying by integrating AI-powered note generation and intelligent learning assistance into a modern web platform.
</p>

<p>
The platform focuses on improving productivity, reducing manual effort, and helping students understand concepts faster through clean organization and AI-driven content generation.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Core Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>AI-generated exam notes and summaries</li>
  <li>Simplified explanations for complex topics</li>
  <li>Fast and responsive user experience</li>
  <li>Modern and clean user interface</li>
  <li>Smart note organization and accessibility</li>
  <li>Responsive design for all devices</li>
  <li>Focused productivity-driven learning environment</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Tech Stack</h2>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Frontend:</strong> React.js / Next.js</li>
  <li><strong>Backend:</strong> Node.js / Express.js</li>
  <li><strong>Database:</strong> MongoDB</li>
  <li><strong>AI Integration:</strong> OpenAI API</li>
  <li><strong>Styling:</strong> Tailwind CSS</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Project Vision</h2>

<p>
ExamNotesAI aims to bridge the gap between traditional studying and AI-powered learning by creating a platform where students can study smarter, revise faster, and improve learning efficiency.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Why ExamNotesAI?</h2>

<p>
Students often spend countless hours creating notes manually and organizing study material. ExamNotesAI automates that workflow using artificial intelligence, allowing users to focus more on understanding concepts rather than spending time formatting and managing notes.
</p>
